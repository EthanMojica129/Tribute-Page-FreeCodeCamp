Twenty One Pilots

The band of a generation

Twenty One Pilots (stylized in all lowercase or as twenty øne piløts) are an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio. Initially a band, the group was formed in 2009 by lead vocalist Tyler Joseph along with Nick Thomas and Chris Salih, who both left in 2011. Since their departure, the line-up has consisted of Joseph and drummer Josh Dun.

  • 2009: Tyler Joseph and his friends, Chris Salih and Nick Thomas creates the band Twenty One Pilot.
  • 2009: The group released their debut, self-titled album, Twenty One Pilots.
  • 2011: Salih and Thomas leave the band. Joshua Dun Joins the band.
  • 2011: The band launchs his second album, Regional at Best.
